## Goal
Upgrade the prototype from a 3D-style browser vertical slice into a polished playable horror demo.

## Non-negotiable story pillars
- Protagonist: Saqer, age 18.
- The resort is Qasr Al-Mirath.
- Zarqa Al-Yamama is a tragic prisoner whose sight powers the CCTV.
- Al-Mudawwin is the jinn/entity of ledgers, debt, and recorded fate.
- The Bellboy is the collector/stalker.
- The Qareen is the player's shadow-self and grows with excessive violence.
- Shoes are core mechanics: identity, path, fate, weapon, distraction.
- Every choice must have different consequences.
- One path is the Survival Way.
- True ending requires offering nothing and refusing the logic of debt.

## Technical requests
1. Keep mobile/tablet/PC support.
2. Keep a no-server playable path.
3. Improve the raycast/3D presentation or migrate to Three.js if desired.
4. Add:
   - Real enemy AI for The Bellboy.
   - Inventory wheel.
   - Save/load.
   - Chapter selection for testing.
   - Audio layers for tension.
   - Better lighting and post-processing.
5. Preserve the story data structure so writers can edit cutscenes without touching engine logic.

## Design requests
- The hotel must react to player habits.
- Choices should affect routes, dialogue, enemies, items, endings.
- Avoid cheap jumpscares; make fear come from consequence, uncertainty, and being watched.
- Maintain Arabic horror identity without making it religiously offensive.
